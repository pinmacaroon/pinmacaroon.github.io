block game datapacks yaay

i have made many origins, and tools to make them. its quite fun to play like that. best with friends on a small lan world.

to play them, you need the origins mod ↗ and the fabric loader ↗ usually on the 1.20.1 to 1.20.4 game versions. (version and dependencies varies by datapck, unless otherwise noted)

if you want to make origins yourself, go on the wiki/docs ↗ of the mod on how to get started! to make development interesting, i am working on a typescript library to generate datapacks ↗ via object oriented programming! bleeding edge tech of 2005!
